Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At yooant tools, we don't collect, track, or store any personal data. This is our core promise to users.

How Our Tools Work

  • Client-Side Processing: All data is processed in your browser
  • No Data Storage: Your input is never sent to our servers or any third party
  • No Cookies: We don't use cookies or tracking pixels
  • No Analytics: We don't track user behavior or tool usage
  • No Ads: We don't serve ads or target you with data

External Resources

Some tools use external libraries (like QR code library from CDN). These are loaded from trusted sources and don't involve data transmission to third parties.

Your Data & Control

  • All data stays in your browser session
  • Refreshing the page clears all data
  • You have complete control over your information
  • No information about you is ever saved

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please visit our About page.
